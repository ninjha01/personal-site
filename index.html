Vape Survey [2021]

This webapp was developed for the Johns Hopkins Sociology Dept as part of an investigation into the impact network effects on vaping. It securely and confidentially collects survey responses, anonymously distributes randomized rewards for completing the survey, and displays the results in a network graph visualiation.
